MAKE IT YOUR OWN

Our Mud Kitchens are designed to encourage outdoor play, inspiring children to feel a connection to nature and develop an appreciation for the encironment in an age where we have become so reliant on technology for entertainment. By providing a nature play area in the comfort of your own surroundings no matter if you have a large garde or a small shared yard, your little onces can enjoy the magic, and excitement of outdoor play, creating new "recipes" with mud, and returning to the basic outdoor learning that nature brings.

We at Ryan's Woodworks specialise in creating beautifully handcrafted mud kitchens using recycled pallets. Each charming mud kitchen is sanded to a smooth "no splinter" finish, then treated with a nourishing coat protecting it from the harsh outdoor elements, making it a great addition to any garden for the little ones to experience sensory, emotional and imaginative play.
